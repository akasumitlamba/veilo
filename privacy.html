Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

Advika Innovate built the VeiloScan app as a service. This service is provided by Advika Innovate and is intended for users aged 13 years and older.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the VeiloScan mobile application and website.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use VeiloScan, we may collect certain personally identifiable information, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

This information may be collected when you create an account, contact support, or use features within the app.

User-Generated Data

The app may store certain information generated by users while using the service, including:

  • User ID information
  • QR codes downloaded or generated within the app
  • Call history within the app
  • Messages or chats sent within the app

Please note that messages and calls within the app are encrypted, and the content of those communications is not accessible to us.

Device and App Usage Information

We may collect limited technical information to ensure the proper functioning of the application, such as:

  • App usage data
  • Crash logs
  • Device information necessary for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect may be used to:

  • Provide and maintain the service
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable communication features such as in-app calling and messaging
  • Improve application functionality and performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse of the service

Third-Party Services

VeiloScan may use trusted third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

These services include:

  • Twilio – for communication services such as messaging or calling
  • Firebase – for analytics, authentication, and backend services
  • Cloudinary – for media storage and management

These third-party providers have their own privacy policies governing how they use such information.

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information using modern security practices. Sensitive communications within the app, such as chats and calls, are encrypted at rest in the database and in transit via TLS (Transport Layer Security).

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Children’s Privacy

VeiloScan is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will remove such information from our servers.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
